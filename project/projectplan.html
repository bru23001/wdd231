Site Plan for 'Your Book Club'

Site Name

Site Name: Your Book Club

Reason for Selection:

  • Creates immediate personal connection with "Your"
  • Clearly communicates the purpose (Book Club)
  • Is memorable and straightforward
  • Available as a domain name
  • Works well for branding and social media handles

Site Purpose

Your Book Club serves as a digital hub for members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints who love literature, helping them discover, connect, and engage with books and fellow readers.

The site provides:

  • Featured book recommendations and reviews
  • Book club meeting organization and discovery
  • Community discussion forums
  • Newsletter subscription for literary updates
  • Member profiles and reading lists
  • Event calendar for book club meetings
  • Online and local book club coordination

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

  • Primary Blue (#00A3FF): Used for header background, primary buttons, links
  • Secondary Navy (#1E2B3A): Used for text, secondary buttons, accents

Accent Colors:

  • Accent Red (#FF6B6B): Used for call-to-action buttons, important alerts
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for background, text on dark backgrounds
  • Light Gray (#F7F7F7): Used for secondary backgrounds, cards

These colors were chosen for their high contrast and readability, with blue representing trust and reliability and red providing emphasis for important elements.
